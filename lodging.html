Glynwood Lodging & Booking Information

Stay with us for the weekend! Meals, activities, and swimming included.

Glynwood farm has limited lodging in three beautiful properties. On-site guests will celebrate with us for drinks & dinner Friday evening, will be provided with a fresh farm breakfast Saturday and Sunday, and have access to a family friendly farm tour and swimming Saturday. Lunch will not be provided, however snacks will be available in the boathouse. Guests are welcome to make lunch in the kitchens of Appledore or the Farmhouse, or head 5 minutes into Cold Spring for many delicious options. Guests will also have access to hiking trails from the farm. Free onsite parking is available.

Rooms will be booked on a first come first serve basis. Bookings will not be considered complete until payment is received in full. If selecting a shared room or a room with a shared bath we will do our best to place you with someone you know and contact you directly to confirm that arrangements are suitable. Shared baths share with one other room only.

All lodging is walkable to the reception. Let us know which type of room you would like (below) and we will place you accordingly. +1's are welcome :)

Off-Site Lodging

All guests are invited to the ceremony and reception Saturday night, but due to capacity limitations, the Friday and Saturday day-time events are reserved for on-site guests. We ask off-site guests to provide for their own transportation.

  • Pig Hill Inn: Bed and Breakfast in Cold Spring, rooms range from $160-$260 a night
  • Hudson House: Riverview Inn located in Cold Spring, rooms range from $190-$285 a night
  • The Dutchess Inn: Located on Main St in Beacon, NY, includes an on-site spa, rooms range from $337-$380 a night

Various hotel chains located in Fishkill, NY, about 25 minutes away.
