Nearby Sites + Scenes

  • Fahnestock Park: An expansive state park with many lakes and trails. Peloton pond is 10 minutes from Glynwood, easily accessible off route 301 and appropriately challenging for children. There are picnic tables at the trailhead looking out over the pond. https://parks.ny.gov/parks/fahnestock/details.aspx Stonecrop Gardens: 12 acres of display gardens and conservatory, tours available, 5 minutes from Glynwood. https://www.stonecrop.org/ Chuang Yen Monastery: Home of the Buddhist Association of the United States and the largest indoor statue of Buddha in the western hemisphere. A pleasant place to visit and walk the grounds, and for those interested, group meditations and a Sunday lunch are available. 10- 15 minutes from Glynwood. https://www.baus.org/en/ Dia Beacon: Dia Art Foundation's collection of art from the 1960s to the present, situated on the banks of the Hudson River in Beacon, New York (another town to explore for shopping/dining/strolling by the Hudson River). https://www.diaart.org/visit/visit-our-locations-sites/dia-beacon-beacon-united-states Cold Spring Village: The nearest town just 10 minutes down the road from Glynwood, full of antique shops, restaurants, and mountain views. Dockside park sits on the river at the end of Main Street with a majestic view of the Hudson Highlands. Other Hudson Valley Towns: Ask for more recommendations if you plan to travel in the Hudson Valley. There are so many beautiful places to visit! GETTING THERE BY CAR: Glynwood is located at the end of Glynwood Road, Cold Spring, NY, off Route 301. For GPS: you can type in "Glynwood Road" into Google maps to reach our road. The entrance on the north side of Route 301, between Route 9 and the Taconic State Parkway. A large “Glynwood” sign is located at the top of the road. Please be sure to enter Glynwood via Glynwood Road and not through Stonecrop Lane. After entering the site, please proceed slowly up Glynwood Road for approximately two miles. You will drive along a dirt road through a wooded area, and then you will then reach a paved area and pass a lake, a number of farmhouses and two large red barns. Continue along the road past these buildings, following signs for the office and parking area. Please call 845-265-3338 if you have any difficulties. BY TRAIN: Metro North commuter service is available from Grand Central Station (New York City) to Cold Spring. Trains leave Grand Central approximately every hour (check schedule or visit Metro North’s train schedule for departure times); fares vary during peak and non-peak hours of travel. Taxi service to or from the train station is not generally available without a reservation. Reservations can be made with the following taxi services: A-1 Transportation: (845) 838-1800 Andy’s Car Service: (845) 891-5807 Clearport Limo & Taxi: (845) 283-7288 BY PLANE: The nearest airports are Stewart Airport in Newburgh (30 minutes driving), Westchester County Airport in White Plains (45 minutes driving) and greater NYC area airports (1.5+ hours driving).
