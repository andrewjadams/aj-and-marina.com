Nearby Sites + Scenes

Fahnestock Park

An expansive state park with many lakes and trails. Peloton pond is 10 minutes from Glynwood, easily accessible off route 301 and appropriately challenging for children. There are picnic tables at the trailhead looking out over the pond.

Stonecrop Gardens

12 acres of display gardens and conservatory, tours available, 5 minutes from Glynwood.

Chuang Yen Monastery

Home of the Buddhist Association of the United States and the largest indoor statue of Buddha in the western hemisphere. A pleasant place to visit and walk the grounds, and for those interested, group meditations and a Sunday lunch are available. 10- 15 minutes from Glynwood.

Dia Beacon

Dia Art Foundation's collection of art from the 1960s to the present, situated on the banks of the Hudson River in Beacon, New York (another town to explore for shopping/dining/strolling by the Hudson River).

Cold Spring Village

The nearest town just 10 minutes down the road from Glynwood, full of antique shops, restaurants, and mountain views. Dockside park sits on the river at the end of Main Street with a majestic view of the Hudson Highlands.

Other Hudson Valley Towns

Ask for more recommendations if you plan to travel in the Hudson Valley. There are so many beautiful places to visit!

Getting There

By Car

Type "Glynwood, Cold Spring" into Google maps to reach the farm. The entrance is off Route 301, between Route 9 and the Taconic State Parkway. A large red “Glynwood” sign is located at the top of the road.

After entering the site, proceed slowly up Glynwood Road for approximately two miles. You will drive along a dirt road through a wooded area, then reach a paved area and pass a lake, a number of farmhouses and two large red barns. Continue along the road past these buildings, following signs for the office and parking area.

There is a new entrance to Glynwood, with a paved road. The old entrance listed above is still accessible, it's just not paved. The new entrance is off Big Woods Road on Rt. 301. It will come up as "big woods trailhead parking - fahnestock, Phillipstown" in google maps. At the bottom of the road is a large sign that says BIG WOODS TRAILHEAD. Take the road all the way to the end where you come to a parking lot, and then turn right into Glynwood. Continue onto a dirt road and follow signs for Perkins House.

By Train

Metro North commuter service is available from Grand Central Station to Cold Spring. Trains leave Grand Central approximately every hour (check schedule or visit Metro North’s train schedule for departure times). Taxi service to or from the train station is not generally available without a reservation. Reservations can be made with the following taxi services:

By Plane

The nearest airports are Stewart Airport in Newburgh (30 minutes driving), Westchester County Airport in White Plains (45 minutes driving) and greater NYC area airports (1.5+ hours driving).
